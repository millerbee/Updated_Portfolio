About Me

Hi and welcome to my portfolio page! My name is Brenda and I am a QA Tester. I graduated Metro State University of Denver with a major in CIS and a minor in Business. After graduation I spent time in the database administration area. After a few years of that I took courses at Villanova Univeristy for Software Testing Principles and Practice. I have been in that field for over 12 years and enjoy not only the detective work behind it but the various tools and technolgies Testers are exposed to including SoapUI, ReadyAPI, Insomnia, Postman, MySQL, MSSQL, Oracle SOA, WireShark, Axiros ACS, Linux and vSphere to name a few.
I am now in DU's Full Stack Web developer Boot Camp learning HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery, which I would like to leverage for Automating in a QA environment.
